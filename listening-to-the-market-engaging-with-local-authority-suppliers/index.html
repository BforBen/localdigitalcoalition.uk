Listening to the market: engaging with local authority suppliers

19 Oct 2016, 2:32 pm

By Jyoti Basuita and Livia Ralph from Government Digital Service

Over the last few months we’ve been investigating how GOV.UK Verify might be extended to local authorities. We recently kicked off our first pilot projects on concessionary travel and parking permit services.

A lot of local authorities, including many of our pilot participants, work directly with suppliers to provide aspects of their services. We want to collaborate with these suppliers and understand how industry can get involved in local authority reuse. In this blog we’ll share how we’ve been doing this, what we’ve found and what’s happening next. Read more on the GDS blog.
