#VerifyLocal service patterns and prototypes

3 Jul 2017

As part of the#VerifyLocal pilots, between 7 and 14 councils have co-designed service patterns for two services: older people's concessionary travel and residential parking permits. The patterns are based on user research done in the discovery and alpha phases of pilots.

Inside each service pattern, you can find links to the common prototype that each #VerifyLocal pilot team has collaboratively produced:

  • Residential parking permits prototype
  • Older people's concessionary travel prototype

To see how these products have evolved, just click on the github links on each page, read over the discussions, and add any comments that have not yet been considered.
