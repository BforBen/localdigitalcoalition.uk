#LocalDigital Waste Service Standards Alpha Showcase
We are pleased to report that over 50 delegates from all over the country joined our Alpha Showcase & Workshop on Oct 23rd. We discussed our progress so far, including business case estimates and technical specs. Check out videos of the day's presentations, including slides, below.

As well as hearing what we had to say, event attendees shared their feedback on our work so far, which is essential for us as we now move into the beta phase. Watch this space!
