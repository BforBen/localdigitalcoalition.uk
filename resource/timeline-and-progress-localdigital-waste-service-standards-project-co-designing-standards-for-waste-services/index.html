Timeline and progress: #LocalDigital Waste Service Standards Project – Co-designing standards for waste services

28 Jul 2015

Have a look through our storify to see the progress our #LocalDigital team has made on the Waste Service Standards Project. We are running this pilot project to help a group of local authorities co-design and implement common data standards for their waste services - standards that could be adopted nationally. It aims to document all work and lessons openly and to help make the business case for standards.
