Guest post: working with GDS on transforming local services

22 Mar 2017, 11:17 am

By Joanne Southern from Northumberland County Council

Joanne and Northumberland County Council’s pilot team

I’m Joanne Southern, I work for Northumberland County Council as a Digital Team Leader. My role is to redesign and transform services within the authority.

We are one of several councils working with GDS on 2 pilot projects to transform our older people’s concessionary travel and residential parking permit services using GOV.UK Verify. Read more on the GDS blog.
